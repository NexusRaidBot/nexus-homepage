Nexus

A Pokémon Go raid coordination bot for Discord

About

Nexus is a Discord Bot that organizes remote raids for Pokémon Go, no matter where you are. It has incorporated a reaction role system, both a generic reaction role and an easy-to-use Pokémon reaction role to subscribe to Pokémon roles, with automatic custom emojis. Raids will be posted with role mentions to notify people, and weather boosted raids are supported by Nexus as well. You can even raid across servers!

Raid Coordination

Trainers can join raids with just one click of the raid pass, and Nexus will instruct Trainers on what to do next. Communication with the raid Host will be handled in dedicated a channel and messages will work across servers automatically!

Trainer Profiles

Trainer profiles work across the entire Nexus network making joining raids simple and painless on any server.

Notifications

A simple reaction role-based notifications system will keep Trainers engaged when new raids are hosted.

Leaderboards

Earn points by hosting and joining raids with Nexus. Points will show in profiles and leaderboards for individual servers allowing communities to reward active Trainers!

Raid Hosting

Raid hosting couldn’t be any easier with Nexus. Host simply upload a screenshot or enter a single command and Nexus walks Trainers through the rest. No more worrying about how to spell Raid Boss names, Yay!

Pokebattler Raid Network

Nexus is integrated with the Pokebattler Raid Network. All raids are sent through the network without any input from the raid host, and Trainers from anywhere in the world will be able to see them and join the Nexus raid directly from the Pokebattler Raid Party app.
